Tribute to Edward Snowden

Edward Snowden is a former intelligence contractor who gained international attention in 2013 when he leaked classified documents revealing the global surveillance programs conducted by the United States National Security Agency (NSA).

Snowden's disclosures exposed the extent of mass surveillance, including the collection of phone records and internet communications of millions of people worldwide. His courage in revealing these practices led to a significant reevaluation of privacy rights and government accountability.

Edward Snowden's actions have had a lasting impact on the conversation around digital privacy and civil liberties. Some see him as a hero for exposing government overreach, while others view him as a traitor. Regardless of one's perspective, there is no denying the profound impact Snowden has had on the ongoing discourse about the balance between national security and individual privacy.
